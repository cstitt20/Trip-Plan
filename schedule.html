Day 2 — Wednesday, July 2
Kennedy Space Center — Bus Tour, Meet an Astronaut, Planet Play 🚀
9:00 AM
Space Arrive at rope drop. Head straight to the 60th Anniversary KSC Bus Tour queue — included with admission. Behind-the-gates access to the launch complex. Look for the collectible bus popcorn bucket — instant souvenir win for a 6-year-old.
Mid Morning
Astronaut Meet Astronaut Charlie Walker — included with admission. Check the KSC app on arrival for exact timing and location. A quick, exciting encounter — perfect for his age.
Late Morning
Space Space Shuttle Atlantis — Shuttle Launch Experience and the slide. Then Rocket Garden and Planet Play — hands-on interactive exhibits built for kids. He'll be running from thing to thing all day.
Evening
Dinner Grab dinner near KSC or cook at the Airbnb. Big day — he's going to crash hard tonight.
📱 Download the KSC app before you go — real-time schedules for astronaut appearances. | 🎟️ Military admission: $72 adult + $62 child = $134 total. Verify via ID.me online in advance or show proof of service at Will Call. | Book tickets at kennedyspacecenter.com before July 2nd.
🏠 Sleep
Airbnb — 1639 Minnie St, Cocoa, FL
Day 3 — Thursday, July 3
Dolphin Catamaran Sail → Jet Ski → Evening Beach
10:30 AM
Catamaran Leave Airbnb, head to Cape Crossing Resort & Marina, 290 Marine Harbor Dr, Merritt Island (~15 min). Arrive by 10:40 AM for check-in and waivers.
11:00 AM
Dolphin Sail Dolphin Adventure Sail — 2-hour catamaran cruise on the Indian River and Intracoastal Waterway. Max 6 passengers so practically a private tour. Dolphins, manatees, sea birds, snacks and drinks included. Son gets a coloring book to keep. Back by 1 PM.
1:30 PM
Lunch Grab lunch near Cocoa Beach before the next adventure.
Afternoon
Jet Ski Cocoa Beach Jet Ski Rentals on the Banana River — calm protected water, no license needed. ~1 hour. Call ahead to reserve.
Evening
Beach Relax on Cocoa Beach, walk the Westgate Pier. Watch the sky for launches. Beachside dinner — last night on the Space Coast.
⛵ Catamaran already reserved — charged $100 on July 1st. Arrive 15 min early for waivers. | 🛥️ Cocoa Beach Jet Ski: (321) 453-8900. Call ahead — July Thursdays fill up. | 🚀 Check spaceflightnow.com for any evening launch window.
🏠 Sleep
Airbnb — 1639 Minnie St, Cocoa, FL
Day 4 — Friday, July 4
Check out → Drive to Orlando → Gatorland → Lake Eola Fireworks 🎆
Morning
Check out Check out of the Airbnb, load the car, drive to Orlando (~1 hr). Drop bags at the Airbnb, then head straight to Gatorland.
Midday
Gators Gatorland — feed alligators, live shows, splash pad, and the zipline. Your adult ticket is free with military ID.
Evening
Fireworks Lake Eola Park downtown Orlando — "Fireworks at the Fountain." Free, nearly 50-year tradition. Live music, food vendors, fireworks over the lake. Arrive by 6–7 PM. Consider rideshare to avoid parking.
💡 Bring a blanket and snacks for Lake Eola. Rideshare recommended for the evening — parking is a nightmare on July 4th.
🏠 Sleep
Airbnb — 1639 Minnie St, Cocoa, FL
Day 5 — Saturday, July 5
Boggy Creek Airboat → Check in to the Signia 🎉
Morning
Airboat Boggy Creek Airboat Adventures in Kissimmee — 1-hour tour. Wild alligators, bald eagles, and turtles in real Florida swampland. Gem mining, butterfly garden, and Boggy Bottoms BBQ on-site.
Afternoon
Check in Check into the Signia by Hilton Bonnet Creek. Drop bags, return the rental car, change, and head straight to the 3-acre lazy river and waterslide.
Evening
Dinner La Luce on-site — your son eats free with an adult entrée. First of three free kid dinners at the Signia.
📞 Call Boggy Creek (407) 344-9550 to book the 1-hour tour — Saturday fills fast. | Diamond status: expect a room upgrade and free breakfast every morning. | Return Hertz car at Signia — no more parking fees.
🏨 Sleep
Signia by Hilton Orlando Bonnet Creek — 14100 Bonnet Creek Resort Ln
Day 6 — Sunday, July 6
Discovery Cove — Swim with Dolphins 🐬
All Day
Discovery Cove Swim with dolphins, snorkel the Grand Reef with rays and tropical fish, hand-feed exotic birds in the aviary, float the Wind-Away River. All-inclusive — breakfast, lunch, unlimited snacks, all gear included. Only 10 minutes from the Signia.
Evening
Dinner Back to the Signia. La Luce dinner — son eats free. Lazy river nightcap if he has any energy left after swimming with dolphins all day.
🐬 Book via discoverycove.com military portal — verify with ID.me. Bring military ID on the day. | $314/person including dolphin swim. | Discovery Cove limits daily guests — book early, July dates sell out fast.
